Meet Denis

A Lifetime of Public Service

Dedicated to transparency, fairness, and efficient use of public funds

Experience That Matters

Denis Bilodeau has spent his career fighting for taxpayers. As President of the Orange Taxpayers Association, he has led efforts to hold government accountable, expose waste, and protect taxpayers' hard-earned dollars.

A graduate of UC Irvine and a licensed Civil Engineer, Denis brings both technical expertise and real-world experience to the Board of Equalization. He understands the challenges California families and small businesses face in today's economy.

  • President, Orange Taxpayers Association
  • City of Orange Councilmember
  • President, Orange County Water District
  • Graduate, University of California, Irvine
  • Licensed Civil Engineer

Key Issues

Fighting for What Matters

Core commitments to California taxpayers

Protect Proposition 13

Your home is one of the few avenues to build lasting wealth. Denis signed the Howard Jarvis Taxpayers Association Pledge to protect Proposition 13 and will always defend your property rights.

No New Taxes

California families and businesses are taxed enough already. Denis signed the Pledge to Oppose Tax Increases and will fight against any attempts to raise your tax burden.

Government Accountability

The Board of Equalization needs skilled, dedicated leaders who stand up for taxpayers—not special interests. Denis will ensure transparency and expose wasteful spending.

Close Unfair Loopholes

Using his expertise in property tax law, Denis will work to close loopholes that benefit special interests while ensuring fair treatment for all California taxpayers.

Support Small Business

California's high taxes are forcing great paying jobs to flee our state. Denis will advocate for policies that help small businesses thrive and create local jobs.

Expert Oversight

With California property assessments exceeding $8.7 trillion, the BOE needs technical expertise. Denis's engineering background ensures informed decision-making.
